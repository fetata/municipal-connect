About Municipal Connect

Municipal Connect is a simple platform created to bring people in the community closer together.

Sometimes small problems remain unsolved not because they are complicated, but because communication is missing. A broken streetlight, a damaged bench, someone looking for help, or someone willing to offer a skill - all of these need one thing: connection.

This platform is designed to make that connection easier.

Through Reports, citizens can signal local issues and help improve their neighborhood.
Through Skills, people can offer their knowledge and support others.
Through the Marketplace, community members can exchange items, post offers, or ask for what they need.

The goal is not just to build a website.
The goal is to encourage cooperation, responsibility, and communication within the municipality.

A stronger community starts with better communication.
Municipal Connect is a step in that direction.
